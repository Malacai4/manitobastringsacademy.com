Chooi Brothers Masterclass 2022 - MSA

Hello Teachers, Parents, and Musicians!

Nikki and Timothy Chooi, world renowned and award winning violinists will be performing in Winnipeg in March and they have agreed to teach two masterclasses for the Manitoba Strings Academy. Masterclass participants are invited to register for one of the masterclasses. Each participant will have up to 20 or 30 minutes to perform their repertoire and receive individualized feedback from one of the clinicians. They will also benefit from hearing the instruction provided to the other masterclass participants.

Location and Dates

The Chooi Brothers will be teaching two in-person masterclasses at St. Andrew’s River Heights United Church, 255 Oak St., Winnipeg:

  1. Monday, March 21, 2022, evening
  2. Wednesday, March 23, afternoon

Participants must choose one masterclass date and indicate this on the registration form, see link below. Exact masterclass times will be confirmed after all registrations have been received.

Clinicians

Canadian violinist Nikki Chooi, praised for his passionate and poetic performances, has established himself as an artist of rare versatility. Described as “vigorous, colorful” by the New York Times, he has received critical acclaim in engagements at Chicago’s Harris Theatre, Philadelphia's Kimmel Center, New York’s Carnegie Hall and Merkin Hall, Vancouver’s Recital Series, as well as appearing as soloist with orchestras across Canada.

Timothy Chooi, is described as Powerful and finely nuanced interpretations, sumptuous sonorities, and a compelling stage presence are just a few of the hallmarks of internationally acclaimed violinist Timothy Chooi. A popular soloist and recitalist, he is sought after as much for his passionate performances as for his wide-ranging repertoire. Recent honors include Second Prize, Belgium’s world-renowned 2019 Queen Elisabeth Competition, First Prize, the 2018 Joseph Joachim International Violin Competition in Germany, and the First Prize, the 2018 Schadt Violin Competition in the USA.

Participants and Repertoire

Group A:
  • Violinists playing at RCM Grade 9 or higher.
  • Participants will select 1-2 works for a total of 12 minutes of playing time. Repertoire must be indicated at the time of registration.
  • Participants will receive 30 min. of individualized time
Group B:
  • Violinists playing at RCM Grade 6-8.
  • Participants will select 1-2 works for a total of 8 minutes of playing time. Repertoire must be indicated at the time of registration.
  • Participants will receive 20 min. of individualized time

Collaborative Pianists

In order to simplify masterclass scheduling, collaborative pianists will be assigned to students. Students will have one 30min. rehearsal with the collaborative pianist (rehearsal times to be arranged by student) and one performance included in their fees. (Works that require additional rehearsals will need to be arranged directly with the pianist, including additional rehearsal fees).

Note: If participants have their own pianist or are planning on performing a solo work, please contact MSA for an amended rate
