SPRINGFEST RECITAL CLASS

SpringFest Recital Class is a one-day event on Saturday April 10th at St. Andrew's River Heights United Church, 255 Oak St. Winnipeg.

The recital class is open to students playing at all levels. Students are expected to perform 1-3 pieces for 10 mins. Students will perform their selections consecutively (with a collaborative pianist if the work requires accompaniment). This performance will be in a live recital format, with no adjudication, where parents are responsible for recording their own children.

After registrations have been received, students will be notified of the exact date and time of their performance. Students will be expected to arrive at least 15 minutes before their assigned time.

Audience

Depending on COVID-19 protocols, there may be opportunity for a small live audience. All COVID-19 health & safety protocols will be strictly adhered to.

Collaborative Pianists

Due to COVID-19 restrictions and health considerations at the venue, the Manitoba Strings Academy has arranged for a collaborative pianist to accompany all performing students. Students will have 1 rehearsal (rehearsal time to be arranged by student) and 1 performance included in their fees.

The collaborative pianists will be Hannah Smith and Leanne Regehr-Lee.

Fees and Policies

Recital Group:
  • Collaborative pianist
  • One rehearsal
  • Performance
Cancellations: There will be a $50 cancellation fee up until March 1, 2021. After this date, no money will be refunded.
