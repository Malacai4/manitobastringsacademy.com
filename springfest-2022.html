Springfest 2022 - MSA

SPRINGFEST 2022

Hello Teachers, Parents, and Musicians!

We are excited to get started with the second year of Manitoba Strings Academy. Last year our first event, SpringFest, was incredibly well received with over 30 musicians participating in the recital class, recording sessions, and online masterclasses. This year we will expand SpringFest to include a wider range of grade levels and chamber groups.

SpringFest

SpringFest is a multi-part event that will allow Manitoba strings students to perform their repertoire in a concert venue. Performances will be video recorded using high quality equipment and shared with the musician and the adjudicator, to be used for feedback in the masterclass.

Solo Performances

Students in Groups A, B, & C will perform their selections consecutively (with a pianist if the work requires accompaniment). At a later date, groups of students will attend an online masterclass which will include individual and group feedback from the adjudicator.

Chamber Performances

Students will form their own chamber groups and perform polished chamber pieces. At a later date, chamber groups will attend an online masterclass which will include group feedback from the adjudicator.

Clinicians

Nationally acclaimed, non-local strings educators will be working with students to provide online adjudication.

Location and Date

All SpringFest events will be held on April 7th, 8th, and 9th, 2022 at St. Andrews River Heights United Church, 255 Oak St., Winnipeg.

After registrations have been received, students will be notified of the exact date and time of their performance. Students will be expected to arrive at least 15 minutes before their assigned time.

Participants

Group A:
  • Students playing at a minimum of Grade 10 RCM level.
  • Students are expected to perform multiple works for up to 30 mins. Works may include a complete sonata or 2-3 separate works of contrasting styles.
Group B:
  • Students playing at a minimum of Grade 8 RCM level.
  • Students are expected to perform multiple works for up to 20 mins. Works may include 2 contrasting movements of a sonata or 2 separate works of contrasting styles.
NEW! Group C:
  • Students playing at a minimum of Grade 6 RCM level.
  • Students are expected to perform one or multiple works for up to 12 mins.
NEW! Chamber Group:
  • Students playing at a minimum of Grade 8 RCM.
  • Groups are expected to play 1-2 movements for up to 15 mins.

Note:

  • Minimum age requirement is 10 years of age.
  • Please choose your preferred group in consultation with your teacher.

Student Repertoire

In order to appropriately schedule students with collaborative pianists, repertoire lists are required at the time of registration. Students in Group C can confirm their repertoire list by Feb. 28th.

Collaborative Pianists

In order to simplify the recording schedules, collaborative pianists will be assigned to students. Setting up rehearsal times with the pianist is the responsibility of the student.

Students in Groups A & B will have 2 rehearsals with the collaborative pianist and 1 performance included in their fees. Works that require additional rehearsals will need to be arranged directly with the pianist, including additional rehearsal fees.

Students in Group C will have 1 rehearsal with the collaborative pianist and 1 performance included in their fees.
